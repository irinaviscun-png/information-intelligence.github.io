Research

Research Areas

Information Theory and Network Science

We explore fundamental questions in information theory with applications to networks, communication systems, and complex systems. Our work includes:

  • Information-theoretic bounds and coding theory
  • Network analysis and graph theory applications
  • Signal processing and distributed systems

Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

Advancing machine learning through information-theoretic perspectives, including:

  • Deep learning theory and optimization
  • Information bottleneck methods
  • Generalization and sample complexity

Interdisciplinary Applications

Applying information and intelligence concepts to diverse real-world problems:

  • Biomedical signal processing
  • Neuroscience and brain-inspired computing
  • Data science and analytics
  • Social networks and collective intelligence
